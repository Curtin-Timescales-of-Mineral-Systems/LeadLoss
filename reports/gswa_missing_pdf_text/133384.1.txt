Zircon morphology
Zircons from this sample are anhedral, and occur as single
crystals or in aggregates. The zircons are up to 150 µm
long, and equant to elongate, with aspect ratios up to
5:1. In cathodoluminescence (CL) images, most crystals
display concentric zoning. A CL image of all zircons is
shown in Figure 1.
