    The date of 2697 ± 6 Ma indicated by the weighted
mean 207 Pb/ 206 Pb ratio of 17 concordant to highly
discordant analyses of 12 zircons is interpreted as the age
of igneous crystallization of the monzogranite. Discordant
analysis 14.1 is interpreted to be of a xenocryst zircon.
The remaining analyses are interpreted to be of sites that
underwent redistribution of radiogenic-Pb during ancient
and recent disturbance episodes.
